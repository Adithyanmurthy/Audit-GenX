The Problem

Insurance fraud costs the industry over 80,000 crore annually

Manual claim processing is slow, inconsistent, and impossible to audit at scale. Claims officers make decisions without structured reasoning, leaving companies exposed to regulatory risk and fraudulent payouts.

Our Solution

A 14-stage AI pipeline that makes every decision explainable, traceable, and audit-ready

Audit GenX processes insurance claims through 14 automated verification stages in under a second. Every stage produces reasoning, a confidence score, and the data source — creating a complete audit trail that satisfies regulators like IRDAI.

For Insurance Companies
  • Process claims in under 1 second instead of 45 minutes
  • Detect fraud with 8 weighted indicators and risk scoring
  • Generate audit-ready documentation for every decision
  • Reduce fraudulent payouts with automated consistency checks
  • Stay compliant with IRDAI audit requirements
For Claims Officers
  • Get clear Approve / Reject / Pending decisions with reasoning
  • See exactly what was checked and why at every stage
  • Receive recommended next actions for each claim
  • Upload documents and auto-extract claim details
  • Watch the pipeline execute in real time

Config-Driven

Change the rules without changing the code

All business logic — coverage rules, fraud thresholds, damage keywords, payout percentages, risk weights — lives in a single JSON configuration file. Different insurance companies can have completely different rules without any code changes.

{
  "coverage_rules": { ... },

  },

14-Stage Processing Pipeline

Every claim passes through 14 automated stages. Each produces reasoning, confidence score, and data source.

#StageWhat It ChecksOutput
1Input ValidationAll 12 fields — types, ranges, requiredValid / errors
2Document VerificationEach of 6 required documentsCompleteness %
3Claim AnalysisIncident type from descriptioncollision / fire / flood / theft
4Damage AssessmentSeverity + repair range checkminor / moderate / major
5Coverage ValidationPolicy type + exclusionsCovered / Not covered
6Policy LimitClaim vs policy maximumWithin / Exceeds
7History CheckPast claims, recent policyRisk flags
8Fraud Detection8 weighted indicatorsScore 0-100
9Consistency CheckAmount vs damage vs policyConsistent / issues
10Risk Scoring6-factor weighted compositeScore 0-100
11DecisionAll prior stagesApproved / Rejected / Pending
12Payout EstimationDamage + deductibleNet payout
13RecommendationsDecision + risk contextActions for officer
14Audit VerificationAll 13 stages for contradictionsConsistent / contradictions
